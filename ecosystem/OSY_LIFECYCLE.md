## 2. THE INTELLIGENCE: AI CONDUCTOR

This is the OSY nervous system. AI does not wait for commands, it **predictively manages scenarios**.

### Protocol A: "Care Loop" (Scenario: Parental Care)
> `INPUT:` [BIO] The sensor detects the arrhythmia of the Father (Node_Father).  
> `PROCESS:` AI analyzes criticality -> Notifies Operator -> Checks doctors' slots.  
> `ACTION:` [BIO] Books a doctor + [OPS] Orders a taxi for his Father.  
> `RESULT:` The problem was resolved before the escalation.

### Protocol B: "Evolution Loop" (Scenario: Development)
> `INPUT:` [EVO] The child's math metric is dropping.  
> `PROCESS:`The AI is looking for a resource inside the Kinship Graph.  
> `ACTION:` He offers Grandpa (Node_Grandfather, an engineer) to teach a lesson.  
> `FINANCE:` [LEGACY] Transfers the bonus to the Grandfather from the family fund.

### Protocol C: "Harmony Loop" (Scenario: Relationship)
> `INPUT:` [BOND] No joint events for > 14 days.  
> `ACTION:` [OPS] Is booking a restaurant + Babysitter for a child.  
> `RESULT:` Restoring the balance in the pair.
